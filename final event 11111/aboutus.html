About Us

Who We Are

We are a global self-service ticketing platform for live experiences that allows anyone to create, share, find and attend events that fuel their passions and enrich their lives. From music festivals, marathons, conferences, community rallies, and fundraisers, to gaming competitions and air guitar contests. Our mission is to bring the world together through live experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for learning, networking, and collaboration through our meticulously curated events. We aim to foster innovation, growth, and professional development among our attendees.
